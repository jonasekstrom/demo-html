About me

I’m Jonas Ekström, a front-end web designer based in Gothenburg, Sweden. I’m into all things web and mobile. I’m currently spending my time at the vocational university to get my degree as a front-end developer.
I spend my days crafting websites. I work for all size of companies from small local businesses right up to huge international brands.
I’m constantly learning web technologies and other design related topics, currently playing around with Javascript and JQuery. I’m a skilled in many areas of design and development.
